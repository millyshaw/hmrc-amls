Responsible people What you need

Who must register as a 'responsible person':

  • anyone who directs the business – this includes sole proprietors, partners, directors and shadow directors
  • anyone who owns or controls at least 25% the business
  • the ‘nominated officer’ – the person who acts as the money laundering reporting officer
Your business must have one nominated officer.

Each responsible person needs to provide their:

  • current name, and any alternative or former names
  • national insurance number, if relevant
  • date of birth
  • passport number, if relevant
  • nationality and country of birth
  • phone number and email address
  • home address
  • position within the business and start date
  • VAT number and Self Assessment number, if relevant
  • details of any relevant experience and training
