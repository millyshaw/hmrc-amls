Business activities What you need

For this section you'll need to know:

  • the anticipated turnover for the next 12 months
  • approximately how many people your business employs
  • approximately how many people work on activities covered by Money Laundering Regulations

Depending on your circumstances, you may also need to know:

  • in which countries your customers live or work
  • your franchisor's name
  • how you record transactions
  • your professional advisor's name and address
  • your professional advisor's HMRC agent code
