More information about these categories

'Accountancy Service Providers' provide professional bookkeeping services, accounts preparation and signing, or tax advice. More information about Accountancy Service Providers.

‘Bill Payment Service Providers’ work on behalf of businesses to handle household bill payments from customers.

'Estate Agency Businesses' introduce people who want to buy or sell property, and facilitate the purchase or sale of property. More information about Estate Agency Businesses.

'High Value Dealers' accept cash payments of €15,000 or more (or equivalent in any currency) in exchange for goods. Estate agents are not classed as High Value Dealers. More information about High Value Dealers.

'Money Service Businesses' exchange currency, transmit money, or cash cheques for their customers. More information about Money Service Businesses.

'Trust or Company Service Providers' form companies. They also provide other services such as acting - or arranging for another person to act - in a certain role within a company. More information about Trust or Company Service Providers.

‘Telecommunications, Digital and IT Payment Service Providers’ manage customer payments for businesses, where the payments are made with devices such as phones, computers, and smart TVs.
