How this is collected

Your employer or pension provider takes off Income Tax before
they pay you. This is called 'Pay As You Earn' (PAYE). Your tax code
tells them how much tax-free income you can have before they deduct Income Tax.

Basic rate Income Tax is usually taken from interest on savings and investments automatically.

Why is this an estimate?

The amount of tax you pay depends on your total income for the whole tax year. We can't give an exact figure because we won't know about all your income until after the tax year ends on 5 April.
