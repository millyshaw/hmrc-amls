Responsible people Guidance

In order to register for anti-money laundering supervision, HMRC must be satisfied that suitable people are running the business.

{{!

The Responsible People section usually takes X minutes to complete.

}}

Who needs to complete this section:

  • anyone who directs the business - this includes sole proprietors, partners, directors and shadow directors
  • ‘beneficial owners’ - anyone who owns or controls at least 25% the business
  • the ‘nominated officer’ - the person who acts as the money laundering reporting officer

What you may need

You need the following information for each person you are registering:

  • current name, and any alternative or former names
  • national insurance number, if relevant
  • passport number, if relevant
  • nationality and country of birth
  • phone number and email address
  • home address and previous addresses, if relevant
  • position within the business and start date
  • VAT number, if relevant
  • Self Assessment number, if relevant
  • details of any relevant experience
  • details of any relevant training
