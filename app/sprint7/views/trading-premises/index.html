Trading premises What you need

We need to know where you trade from, and where your agents trade from. An agent is an individual or business that works on behalf of your business, to offer your services.

For each of your trading premises, you'll need:

  • your trading name
  • your trading address
  • whose premises they are
  • date you will start trading from these premises
  • whether or not it's a residential address

If your business uses agents, you'll need the following information for each one:

  • your agent's registered business name
  • the type of tax your agent pays
  • your agent's business structure
