High Value Dealer What you need

For this section you'll need to know:

  • what your business will sell
  • how you'll sell goods
  • if you will be buying or selling duty-suspended excise goods
  • if your business has already accepted cash payments of €15,000 or more (or the equivalent in another currency)
  • if you are able to automatically identify linked cash payments of €15,000 or more (or the equivalent in another currency)
  • if you expect to receive cash payments of €15,000 from customers you've not met in person (or the equivalent in another currency)
  • what proportion of your turnover you expect to come from cash payments over €15,000 (or the equivalent in another currency)
