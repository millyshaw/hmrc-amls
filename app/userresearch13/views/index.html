{{! Service }} Register for anti-money laundering supervision

Who needs to register:

  • Accountancy Service Providers
  • Estate Agency Businesses
  • High Value Dealers
  • Money Service Businesses
  • Trust or Company Service Providers
  • Bill Payments Service Provider
  • Telephone, Digital and IT Payments Services

Not sure if you need to register?

Before you start

The fee for registering is:

  • £100 to submit the application
  • £110 for each responsible person
  • £110 for each trading premises

When you complete the registration you'll be given the full amount you need to pay.

You have 28 days to complete the registration. You can save your progress and return to it later.
