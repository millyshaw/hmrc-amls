Trading premises What you need

For each of your trading premises, you'll need to know:

  • the trading name and address
  • whose premises they are
  • the date you'll start trading from these premises
  • if it's a residential address
If you use agents, we also need to know where your agents trade from. An agent is an individual or business that works on behalf of your business, to offer your services. An employee is not an agent.
